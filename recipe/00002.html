Crockpot - Pulled Pork

This succulent pulled pork dish will not only amaze your taste-buds, but also your eyes when you see it's only six steps! Make as mild or as hot as you'd like - This slow cooker creation is sure to please!

PREP TIME: 10 m
READY IN: 8 h
COOK TIME: 8 h
SERVES: 12

Ingredients

•

Pork Shoulder - 4 lbs

•

Paprika - 2 tbs

•

Brown Sugar - 2 tbs

•

Black Pepper - 1 tsp

•

Cayenne Pepper - 1 tsp

•

Garlic Powder - 1 tsp

•

Onion Powder - 1 tsp

•

Kosher Salt - 1 tsp

•

Apple Cider Vinegar - 1/4 cups

•

Water - 3/4 cups

•

Hot Sauce of Choice

Instructions

1.

Mix together all of the dried spices and rub into all sides of the pork.

2.

Place vinegar and water in the bottom of slow cooker.

3.

Carefully add pork (so you don't wash off spices) in the certer of the slow cooker. Finally, add hot sauce of choice.

4.

Cover and cook on low for about 8 hours. However, 6 hours into the cooking process taste broth to determine the desire for additional hot sauce.

5.

Remove pork from slow cooker and shred (remove any large fatty pieces and discard).

6.

Add juices from slow cooker as desired.
