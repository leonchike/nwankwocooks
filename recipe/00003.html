PREP TIME: 0 m
READY IN: 30 m
COOK TIME: 30 m
SERVES: 10

Ingredients

•

Baby Spinach - 3 lbs

•

Whole Milk - 1 1/2 cups

•

Heavy Cream - 1 cup

•

Unsalted Butter - 1/2 stick

•

All-Purpose Flour - 1/4 cup

•

Freshly Grated Nutmeg - 1/8 tsp

•

Vidalia Onion - 1 small

•

Spices to taste - Nutmug, Garam Masala, Salt, Black Pepper, Tarragon, Ghost Pepper hot sauce, Cumin

Instructions

Cooking the Spinach

1.

Cook spinach in 2 batches in 1 inch of boiling salted water in an 8-quart pot, stirring constantly, until wilted, 1 to 2 minutes.

2.

Drain in a colander and rinse under cold running water until cool. Squeeze small handfuls of spinach to remove as much moisture as possible, then coarsely chop.

3.

Heat milk and cream in a small saucepan over moderate heat, stirring, until warm. Meanwhile, cook onion in butter in a 3-quart heavy saucepan over moderately low heat, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 4 minutes.

4.

Whisk in flour and cook roux, whisking for 3 minutes. Add warm milk mixture in a fast stream, whisking constantly to prevent lumps, and simmer, whisking, until thickened, 3 to 4 minutes.

5.

Stir in nutmeg, spinach, and salt and pepper to taste and cook, stirring, until heated through.
