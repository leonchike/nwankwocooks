PREP TIME: 12 h
READY IN: 15 h
COOK TIME: 3 h
SERVES: 8

Ingredients

•

Chicken Thighs - 4 pieces

•

Chicken Breasts - 4 pieces

•

Baking Pan - not flat

•

Salt

•

Pepper

•

Adobo Seasoning

•

Onion Powder

•

Pepper

•

Parsley Flakes (optional)

•

Butter

•

Apple Cider Vinegar

Instructions

Prepping the Chicken

1.

Place the chicken pieces in a big bowl and add 1 tablespoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of Apple Cider Vinegar.

2.

Add water to cover all the pieces of chicken within the bowl.

3.

Allow to soak for 30 minutes to an hour.

4.

Rinse well afterwards. Pour the water out essentially.

5.

Season the chicken with garlic salt (about 2 tablespoons), adobo seasoning (about 2 tablespoons), onion powder and pepper as required. Add parsley flakes as a last step.

6.

Place chicken in the refrigerator so it marinates overnight.

Cooking

1.

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

2.

Place the chicken in a baking pan. I’d recommend placing the skin down for the thighs.

3.

Add a quarter stick of butter, I’d recommend cutting it into small pieces and strategically place then around the chicken.

4.

Add 1/2 cup of water to the pan and 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar for flavor.

5.

Cover the baking pan in aluminum foil.

6.

Bake the chicken on one side for an hour. After the initial hour remove the aluminum foil. Turn the chicken so the other side is down. Bake for another hour.

7.

After two hours of cooking they should be done!
