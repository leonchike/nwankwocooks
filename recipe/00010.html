Chicken and Dumplings - Slow Cooker

The definition of comfort food, what this dish is missing in veggies makes up in dumplings (and more dumplings).

PREP TIME: 10 m
READY IN: 6 h
COOK TIME: 6 h
SERVES: 8

Ingredients

•

Boneless Chicken Breast Halves - 4 pieces

•

Butter - 2 tbs

•

Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup - 2 (10.75 ounce) cans

•

Onion - 1, finely diced

•

Refrigerated Biscuit Dough, 2 (10 ounce) packages, torn into pieces

•

Salt and Pepper

•

Water ~ 2 cups

Instructions

1.

Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker, and fill with enough water to cover. **Put in the least amount of water necessary to just barely cover it. It will need lots of room for the dumplings because they puff up when cooked and can make the juice overflow from the pot.

2.

Cover and cook for 5 to 6 hours on High. About 30 minutes before serving, place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center.

3.

All done, it should smell amazing.
