Mashed Potatoes

PREP TIME: 15 m
READY IN: 1 h
COOK TIME: 45 m
SERVES: 8

Ingredients

•

Potatoes - 5 lbs

•

Butter - 1 stick

•

Whole Milk - to taste

•

Water ~ 8 qts

Instructions

1.

Start boiling a large pot of water (~8qts).

2.

Peel the potatoes.

3.

Cut peeled potatoes into fourths.

4.

Boil potatoes until soft (~30 min).

5.

Pour into colander.

6.

Pour potatoes back into pot.

7.

Mash with hand masher.

8.

Cut up stick of butter and add into potatoes, continue mashing.

9.

Pour milk a little at a time while mashing until desired consistency.

10.

Add salt - More salt than you think you need.

11.

Keep tasting/salting until 100% perfection.

12.

Done!
