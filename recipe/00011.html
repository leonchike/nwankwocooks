Mashed Potatoes

Make your aunties jealous when everyone raves about these potatoes! These are fluffy, buttery, and ready for your next potluck, holiday, or Tuesday night!

PREP TIME: 15 m
READY IN: 1 h
COOK TIME: 45 m
SERVES: 8

Ingredients

•

Potatoes - 5 lbs

•

Butter - 1 stick

•

Whole Milk - to taste

•

Water ~ 8 qts

•

Salt - to taste

Instructions

1.

Start boiling a large pot of water (~8qts).

2.

Peel the potatoes. Cut peeled potatoes into fourths. Boil potatoes until soft (~30 min).

3.

Pour potatoes into a colander. Pour potatoes back into pot.

4.

Mash with hand masher. Cut up stick of butter and add into potatoes, continue mashing. Pour milk a little at a time while mashing until desired consistency.

5.

Add salt - More salt than you think you need. Keep tasting/salting until 100% perfection.
