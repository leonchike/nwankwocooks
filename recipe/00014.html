PREP TIME: 10 m
READY IN: 40 m
COOK TIME: 30 m
SERVES: 4

Ingredients

•

Chicken Thighs - 4 pieces

•

Shallots - 1 piece

•

White Wine - 1/4 cup

•

Vegetable Oil - 2 tbs

•

Butter - 1/2 stick

•

Chicken Stock - 1/4 cup

•

Lemon Juice - 2 tbs

•

Salt and Pepper

•

Spices - Parsley, Gram

Instructions

The Chicken

1.

Heat a cast iron with vegetable oil.

2.

Salt, pepper and spice the chicken.

The Fond

1.

Roast at 450℉ for 45-55 minutes. This is a really great time to make the garlic sauce. See the recipe below!

2.

Remove chicken from oven and let it rest for at least 10 minutes. The skin should be crispy.
