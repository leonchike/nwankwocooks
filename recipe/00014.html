Baked Chicken with Fond

Have you ever wanted to make a meal that costs $3 but tastes like $30? You've come to the right recipe. Benefits include using words like "fond" and "shallots."

PREP TIME: 10 m
READY IN: 40 m
COOK TIME: 30 m
SERVES: 4

Ingredients

•

Chicken Thighs - 4 pieces

•

Shallots - 1 piece

•

White Wine - 1/4 cup

•

Vegetable Oil - 2 tbs

•

Butter - 1/2 stick

•

Chicken Stock - 1/4 cup

•

Lemon Juice - 2 tbs

•

Salt and Pepper

•

Spices - Parsley, Gram

Instructions

The Chicken

1.

Heat a cast iron with vegetable oil at a medium high setting & preheat the oven to 400℉.

2.

Salt, pepper and spice the chicken.

3.

Place chicken skin side down on skillet for about 4 minutes. Don't move the chicken around on the skillet.

4.

Flip the chicken to the other side for about 3 more minutes.

5.

Place the chicken in the oven for about 35 minutes, or until the internal temperature is 160℉.

6.

Begin the fond when the chicken is about 5 minutes to completion. Don't get rid of the fond leftover on the skillet.

The Fond

1.

Cut the shallots into really small pieces.

2.

Heat the skillet and the fond from cooking the chicken.

3.

Place the cut shallots into the heated pan until slightly browned. Add white wine, chicken stock, and the freshly chopped thyme and sage to the pan. With the liquid almost entirely evaporated. Afterwhich, add the butter and lemon juice.

4.

Continue to cook over medium heat until the sauce is thick and the butter is melted down.

5.

That's it. It's time to plate and cover the chicken with the fond.
