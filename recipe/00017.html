PREP TIME: 5 m
READY IN: 25 m
COOK TIME: 20 m
SERVES: 4

Ingredients

All the recepes

•

Salmon - 2 lbs

•

Olive Oil

•

Salt and Pepper

•

Paprika

•

Basil

•

Gram

•

Onion - 1/3

•

Aluminum Foil

Instructions

Perparing the Dish for Baking

1.

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.

2.

Cut the Salmon into 3 smaller pieces if it comes as one large piece. Lay out a piece of foil that is big enough to hold and cover the salmon fillets. Lightly rub olive oil on all the surfaces of the salmon fillet. Add a dash of salt and pepper to all surfaces of the fillet. Sprinkle a dash of paprika and basil to the fillet.

3.

Wrap the fillets in the aluminum foil, skin side down. Cut the onion into small pieces and place around the salmon in the foil. Place the salmon in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the thickest point is 125 degrees for medium rare or 145 for well done.

4.

Once its internal temperature reaches 145 remove from oven. Sprinkle a dash of lemon juice to serve. Remember to not include the onion pieces, they are used for flavor during the baking process.
