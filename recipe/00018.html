Baby Bok Choy with Garlic

This Baby Bok Choy comes out tender and savory - Perfect to pair with your favorite chicken or pasta dish!

PREP TIME: 5 m
READY IN: 20 m
COOK TIME: 10-15 m
SERVES: 4-6

Ingredients

•

Unsalted cutter - 1/8 cup

•

Minced garlic - 5 teaspoons or to taste

•

Baby bok choy - 12-14 small sized

•

Low sodium chicken broth - 1.5 cups

Instructions

1.

Melt butter in heavy large skillet over medium heat.

2.

Add minced garlic and saute about 2-3 minutes until barely golden/brown.

3.

Add bok choy and chicken broth and simmer until bok choy is tender. Cover the skillet while the bok choy cooks. Turning occasionally, cook for about 12 minutes.

4.

Season with salt and pepper once the cooking is done.

5.

Move the bok choy and it's broth to a bowl to eat!
